## 📊 AI Analytics Dashboard (Admin)
The Admin Dashboard provides real-time insights into tourism safety and platform health.

### Key Charts & Metrics:
- **Regional Scam Trends**: Line chart showing scam volume by city/district over time.
- **Safety Score Distribution**: Heatmap visualization of safety coverage across geographic regions.
- **SOS Incident Statistics**: Real-time ticker of active SOS events and resolution times.
- **Cultural Participation**: Bar charts showing user engagement with local festivals and workshops.
- **AI Prediction Accuracy**: Confidence levels of the Scam Detection Engine's risk scores.

*Implementation: React.js with TailwindCSS and Chart.js/D3.js integration.*

## 🚀 Scalability & Engineering Realism
- **PostGIS-Enabled**: Geolocation is the backbone; spatial indexing ensures millisecond response times for "Nearby Scams".
- **Edge Intelligence**: Heavy data like maps and cultural images are served via CloudFront CDN.
- **Resilient SOS**: The SOS service is isolated from the main API to ensure 99.999% uptime during surge traffic.
